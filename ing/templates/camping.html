Rules

Access to pools and wading pool
Pools and splash pad hours are from 10:00 a.m. to 7:00 p.m.
T-shirts, denim clothing or cotton are not allowed in the pools.
It is necessary to wear appropriate clothing for the use pool.
Do not run near the pool r play rough.
No diving.

Music
Music is allowed from 10:00 a.m. - 10:00 p.m.
During the day the volume of the music should be moderate and just for your camp.
Live music is prohibited (musical groups, example: Band)

Motorcycles
Motorcycles, ATV´s y UTV’s hours are from 9:00a.m. to 7:00p.m.
Motorcycles, ATV´s and UTV’s are only allowed on the track.
For no reason should motorcycles travel more than 10km/hr to leave their campto the track.

Glass Bottles
It is not allowed to introduce glass bottles or containers into the pools area.

12 and under
Must always have to be accompanied by an adult.

Firewood
It is not allowed to cut firewood, brenches,shrubs,trees,etc.

Paints
The use of paints or graffiti is not allowed.

Drugs
It is not allowed to introduce or use drugs.

Fireworks
It is not allowed to introduce or detonate Fireworks.

Firearms
It is not allowed introduce or detonate firearms,pellets,ammunition or paintball.

Camping
Camping or parking in cabin áreas is not allowed.

Horses/Ponies
It ir not allowed to run or trot with horses or ponies, they are only for walking.

Grills
Two grills are not allowed when parking a single car.

Benches
It is not allowed to move benches without authorization.

Pets
The owner must be responsible for his pet. If he chases farm animals, he should be put on a leash. Inside the cabins, pets must sleep in their own bed or cage. It is your obligation to clean any area your pet messes up.
