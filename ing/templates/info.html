Rules

Access to pools and wading pool
Pools and splash pad hours are from 10:00 a.m. to 7:00 p.m.
T-shirts, denim clothing or cotton are not allowed in the pools.
It is necessary to wear appropriate clothing for the use pool.
Do not run near the pool r play rough.
No diving.

Music
Music is allowed from 10:00 a.m. - 10:00 p.m.
During the day the volume of the music should be moderate and just for your camp.
Live music is prohibited (musical groups, example: Band)

Motorcycles
Motorcycles, ATV´s y UTV’s hours are from 9:00a.m. to 7:00p.m.
Motorcycles, ATV´s and UTV’s are only allowed on the track.
For no reason should motorcycles travel more than 10km/hr to leave their campto the track.

Glass Bottles
It is not allowed to introduce glass bottles or containers into the pools area.

12 and under
Must always have to be accompanied by an adult.

Firewood
It is not allowed to cut firewood, brenches,shrubs,trees,etc.

Paints
The use of paints or graffiti is not allowed.

Drugs
It is not allowed to introduce or use drugs.

Fireworks
It is not allowed to introduce or detonate Fireworks.

Firearms
It is not allowed introduce or detonate firearms,pellets,ammunition or paintball.

Camping
Camping or parking in cabin áreas is not allowed.

Horses/Ponies
It ir not allowed to run or trot with horses or ponies, they are only for walking.

Grills
Two grills are not allowed when parking a single car.

Benches
It is not allowed to move benches without authorization.

Pets
The owner must be responsible for his pet. If he chases farm animals, he should be put on a leash. Inside the cabins, pets must sleep in their own bed or cage. It is your obligation to clean any area your pet messes up.
He should bring his vaccination record and should check that he is protected against fleas and ticks.

FAQ

Where is it located?
Km. 105 Carretera libre Tecate – Mexicali.

How far are you from Tijuana?
One hour away.

How far are you from Mexicali?
One hour away.

How far are you from Tecate?
25 minutes away

Are you on Google Maps?
Yes. Go to Google Maps.

Does children count as people in the cabins?
Yes, children 7 years and older count as people on a cabin.

Do you accept pets?
Yes, pets are allowed as long as the owner takes responsibility and bring their leash. To sleep in the cabin they need a bed or cage

Can you enter with alcoholic beverages?
Yes, glass containers are not allowed in the pool area.

Do you rent motorcycles?
No.

Do you rent horses?
Yes.

Is there restaurants or stores?
We have Al Café Market store (7:00 a.m. – 10:00 p.m.), we sell coffee, wine, beer, meat, charcoal, ice cream, burritos, nachos, hot dogs, sandwiches, water, fruit, vegetables, sweets, snacks, sodas , juices, snacks, ice, dairy, etc.

Do the cabins have refrigerator?
All cabins have refrigerators. Only the Love Shack camper does not have a refrigerator.

Do the cabins have fans or air conditioning?
Grand Deluxe is air-conditioned.
Chalet Campestre, Las Lilis Deluxe, Los Rigos Deluxe, Los Padres, Romance Deluxe and CampoVerde (Classic, Family and Plus) have ceiling fans.

Do the cabins have light?
Yes.

Do the cabins have electricity?
All cabins have electricity with the exception of the Love Shack camper.
Are there lifeguards on the pool?
No, there are no lifeguards.

Is there Wi-Fi?
Yes, there is Wi-Fi in reception. Cabins with Wi-Fi are: Country Chalet, Las Lilis Deluxe, Los Rigos Deluxe, Las Machis Deluxe, Las Chelas, Los Padres, Romance, Romance Plus, Romance Deluxe, Club House, Dos Cielos, Hermana Grande and Grand Deluxe.

Are there showers with hot water and bathrooms for those who camp?
Yes.

Do you have to book to camp?
No, you pay upon arrival.

Are there benches and grills for those who camp?
Yes.

Do camping areas have electricity?
No.

Do camping areas have grass?
No.

¿Is there electricity in some area to inflate mattresses or charge cell phones?
Yes, in the pool area.

¿Can you enter the pool with a t-shirt?
No, only with swimwear.

¿What are the hours for music?
From 10:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m.

¿Can I bring my motorcycles?
Yes, they should only ride in areas intended for motorcycling.

¿Can you break piñatas?
Yes.

¿Can you bring a musical group?
No

Do the cabins have TV?
The Las Lilis Deluxe, Los Rigos Deluxe, Las Machis Deluxe, Las Chelas, Los Padres, Grand Deluxe and Club House cabins have T.V.

Do you accept card payments?
Yes, we have a terminal at the ranch.
