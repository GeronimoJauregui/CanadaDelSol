Rules

Pool Access
Pool hours are from 10:00a.m, to 7:00p.m.
The use of t-shirts,denim or cotton clothing is not allowed in the pool.
It is necessary to wear appropriate clothing for the use pool.
Do not run near the pool r play rough.
No diving.
Access to the pool is available from March 19 to October 31, 2022

Music
Music is allowed from 10:00 a.m. - 10:00 p.m.
During the day the volume of the music should be moderate and just for your camp.
Live music is prohibited (musical groups, example: Band)

Motorcycles
Motorcycles, ATV´s y UTV’s hours are from 9:00a.m. to 7:00p.m.
Motorcycles, ATV´s and UTV’s are only allowed on the track.
For no reason should motorcycles travel more than 10km/hr to leave their campto the track.

Glass Bottles
It is not allowed to introduce glass bottles or containers in the pool area.

12 and under
Must always have to be accompanied by an adult.

Firewood
It is not allowed to cut firewood, brenches,shrubs,trees,etc.

Paints
The use of paints or graffiti is not allowed.

Drugs
It is not allowed to introduce or use drugs.

Fireworks
It is not allowed to introduce or detonate Fireworks.

Firearms
It is not allowed introduce or detonate firearms,pellets,ammunition or paintball.

Camping
Camping or parking in cabin áreas is not allowed.

Horses/Ponies
It ir not allowed to run or trot with horses or ponies, they are only for walking.

Grills
Two grills are not allowed when parking a single car.

Benches
It is not allowed to move benches without authorization.

Pets
If your pet chases the farm animals, you should put it on a leash. Inside the cabins, pets must sleep in their bed or cage. It is your obligation to clean any areas that your pet gets dirty.

Cancellation and reservation policies.

You must bring your reservation confirmation printed.

In case you cannot attend on the reserved date, you must give notice 15 days in advance nd there is a 25% charge of the total amount.

In case of no show, there are no refunds or changes. We promote a family environment.

In case of violation of this, the company may demand de eviction of the facilities witgout any refunt.

In case of refusal to comply this, we will inform the competent authorities.
